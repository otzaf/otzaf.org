Otzaf

Otzaf is a software house based in Brighton, UK.

Vision

We want to provide a suite of polished open-source software(-as-a-service) solutions.

Our current plan: create open source software can be downloaded and installed by anyone locally on their machines, with an option to use our hosted service for a small monthly-recurring cost — this helps us keep the lights on.

Transparency

Most of our work is done in the public eye, and can be contributed to by anyone. Our roadmap is publicly available here.
